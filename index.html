Advantages of procrastination

Despite procrastination is thought to be something wrong there are some indisputable advantages of this really important process:

  • Energy accumulation
  • Structuring of knowledge
  • Brightening the mood that in turn causes:
    • Better performance
    • More effective team work
    • Less risk of injury or death
Kinds of procrastination

There are a lot of kinds of procrastination. However the most valuable are:

  • Reading books
  • Watching anime
  • Walking the streets

After all procrastination is an individual process. Various folks can procrastinate differently and have the most effect from other kinds of procrastination.

Reading books

You can read different books, and in some of them you can find such phrases:

$ git clone https://github.com/libgit2/libgit2 mylibgit

$ git commit -m "Story 182: fix benchmarks for speed"

$ git log --pretty="%h - %s" --author='Junio C Hamano' --since="2008-10-01" \ --before="2008-11-01" --no-merges -- t/

$ git restore --staged CONTRIBUTING.md

$ git remote show origin

Don't be afraid! They're still just books. They won't hurt you.

Watching anime

The Internet is full of tons of anime. And it's wonderful! Because everybody can find relevant anime be it shōnen or shōjo, ecchi or isekai, or mecha, or something else.

Walking the streets

Walking the streets is nice, but if you have any forest in the neighboorhood go to forest and walk there. Forest is better than the streets.
